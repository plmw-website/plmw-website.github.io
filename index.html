The Programming Languages Mentoring Workshop

What is PLMW?

A workshop designed to encourage and prepare students for research careers in programming languages, with:

  • Expert speakers
  • Networking opportunities
  • Career guidance

Who can attend?

PLMW is open to students from all around the world, from undergraduates to PhD students, who are interested in programming languages.

Our sponsors can provide financial support for your flight and hotel. We help you obtain a travel visa.

Attend a research conference

PLMW includes entry to a colocated programming languages research conference (POPL, PLDI, ICFP, or OOPSLA).

You'll see presentations of new research and meet researchers in the field.
