The Programming Languages Mentoring Workshop

What is PLMW?

A workshop designed to encourage and prepare students for research careers in programming languages, with:

  • Expert speakers
  • Networking opportunities
  • Career guidance

Who can attend?

PLMW is open to students from all around the world, from undergraduates to PhD students, who are interested in programming languages.

Our sponsors can provide financial support for your flight and hotel. We help you obtain a travel visa.

The colocated conference

PLMW includes entry to a colocated programming languages research conference (POPL, PLDI, ICFP, or OOPSLA).

You'll see presentations of new research and meet researchers in the field.

FAQ

I'm not from a famous university. Can I still get funding? Absolutely! We want to encourage students from all backgrounds to attend PLMW. We have a limited number of travel grants available for students who need financial support, and we prioritize students who would not otherwise be able to attend the conference.

I have a disability. Can I attend? Yes! The conference venue is chosen to be accessible to all attendees. If you have any specific requirements, please let us know when you register so that we can make sure you have everything you need.

I don't know anything about programming language theory. That's okay! PLMW is designed to be accessible to students at all levels. You don't need to be an expert in programming languages to attend. The talks are designed to introduce you to new ideas and help you understand the current state of the field.

Will there be an exam? No! PLMW is not a course, and there are no exams. The goal of the workshop is to introduce you to new ideas and help you prepare for a career in programming languages research. There are no grades, and you don't need to have any prior knowledge to attend.

My {gender, ethnicity, nationality, sexual orientation, age, disability status} is X. Will I be the only one? PLMW is an inclusive international event, and we welcome students from all backgrounds. For instance, some of the best PL researchers and many past PLMW speakers and attendees are women. We strive to create a welcoming and supportive environment for all attendees. What topics are covered at PLMW? Topics include research areas in programming languages, career development, networking, and skills for succeeding in graduate school and research.

Is there a fee to attend PLMW? No, PLMW is free to attend, but you may need to register for the associated conference. This cost is covered if you apply for and receive funding.

Are travel grants available? Yes, PLMW offers travel grants to help cover the costs of attending the workshop.

Will I have a chance to interact with established researchers? Yes! PLMW includes a mentoring session where you can meet and talk with established researchers in the field. This is a great opportunity to ask questions, get advice, and make connections. You can also meet researchers at the associated conference.

Is there a dress code for PLMW? No, there is no dress code for PLMW. You should wear whatever makes you feel comfortable. At the associated conference, more people wear t-shirts than suits.

Do I need to prepare anything before attending? No, you do not need to prepare anything before attending PLMW.

What should I bring to PLMW? You do not need to bring anything to PLMW. If you want to take notes, you can bring a pen and notebook if you like, but you can also get those from the sponsor booths at the conference.
